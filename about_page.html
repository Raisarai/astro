ASTRO is an online platform concerned with modern migration. In the last 50 years the number of young professionals moving away from their home country for reasons other than desperation has increased hugely and, because of the presence of the internet and a new space in which to live, it is hard to know how much change is to be expected when moving to a new place and where one’s community lies. ASTRO is speaking to a relatively new class of people—the internationals—
